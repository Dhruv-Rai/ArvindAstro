✦ Common Questions

Questions About Kundali Reading

Everything you need to know before booking your Kundali analysis session.

Still have questions?
Reach out on WhatsApp and we'll respond within a few hours.
Do I need my exact birth time? ▾
Exact birth time gives the most accurate Ascendant and house placements. Even without it we can do meaningful readings using Moon chart. Share the closest approximate time you know.
How long is the session? ▾
Most Kundali sessions run 60–75 minutes. A detailed PDF report is delivered within 24 hours after the session.
Can I book from outside India? ▾
Absolutely. We regularly serve clients in the USA, UK, UAE, Canada and Singapore. Sessions via Google Meet. International card payments accepted.
What languages are sessions in? ▾
Hindi and English both available. You can choose your preferred language at time of booking.
Is my data kept private? ▾
Yes — 100%. Your birth details and session content are never shared with any third party under any circumstances.
