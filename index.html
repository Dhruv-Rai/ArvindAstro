Who Am I

Guiding Souls Through
Celestial Wisdom

A well known Vastu Expert & Astrologer, belongs to the spiritual and heritage city of India, Varanasi.He was keen to learn astrology in his childhood days because of the religious atmosphere at his home. At a very early stage of his life, he started in-depth study & research of vastu and astrology. And around the age of nineteen he started advising people based on astrology and vastu. He achieved expertise in this field by undergoing a unique form of education & training, based on the Guru-Shishya traditional methodology, under Dr.Kamlakant Pandey who was a renowned lecturer in Astrology at Banaras Hindu University.

His mentor Sri.Avdhesh Jha (Tantrik Guru) taught him the rituals and beneficial remedies based on occult science as like Tantra vidya. Honorable spiritual guru Sri.Sadanand Shastri accepted him as his pupil and gave him the knowledge of yoga and meditation. At present he is a very professional astrologer who provides his services to his clients on prior appointment.

Still have questions?

Do I need my exact birth time for a reading? ▾
Exact birth time gives the most accurate Ascendant and house placements. However, even without it, we can do meaningful readings using Moon chart and other techniques. Please share the closest approximate time known.
How long is a typical consultation? ▾
There is no fixed duration for a consultation. The session continues until your concerns are fully discussed and addressed. You are welcome to ask as many questions as needed during this time.
Can I book a session from outside India? ▾
Absolutely. We regularly serve clients in the USA, UK, UAE, Canada and Singapore. Sessions via Google Meet work seamlessly across time zones. Payment via international cards accepted.
What languages are consultations available in? ▾
Guruji offers consultations in both Hindi and English to ensure clear and comfortable communication.
Is my data and information kept private? ▾
Yes, 100%. Your birth details, questions, and session recordings are never shared with any third party under any circumstances. We take client privacy very seriously.
